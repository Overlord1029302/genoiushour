Tulip

Scientific Name: Tulipa

Family: Liliaceae

Native Range: Native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

Description: Recognizable for their cup-shaped flowers, tulips bloom in a variety of vibrant colors, making them a favorite in spring gardens.

Care Tips: Prefer well-drained soil and full sun. Plant bulbs in the fall for spring blooming.

Interesting Fact: Tulips were so valuable in the Netherlands during the 17th century that they triggered "Tulip Mania," a period of speculation that led to financial ruin for many investors.
