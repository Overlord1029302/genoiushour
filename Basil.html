Basil

Scientific Name: Ocimum basilicum

Family: Lamiaceae

Native Range: Native to tropical regions of central Africa and Southeast Asia.

Description: A popular culinary herb known for its fragrant leaves, which are widely used in cooking, especially in Italian dishes.

Care Tips: Prefers full sunlight, well-drained soil, and regular watering. Pinch off tops to encourage bushier growth.

Interesting Fact: Basil is considered a sacred herb in some cultures and is often associated with love and protection.
