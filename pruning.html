Pruning

What It Is: Cutting away dead or damaged parts of a plant.
Why It's Cool:
  • Boosts Growth: Helps the plant focus on new leaves and flowers.
  • Keeps It Neat: Shapes the plant and improves airflow.
How to Do It: Use clean scissors to snip off dead leaves just above a leaf node.
