Rose

Scientific Name: Rosa

Family: Rosaceae

Native Range: Various regions across the Northern Hemisphere.

Description: Known for their beautiful blooms and fragrant scent, roses come in a wide variety of colors and sizes. They are often used in gardens, as cut flowers, and for special occasions.

Care Tips: Require full sun and well-drained soil. Regular pruning and deadheading encourage new blooms.

Interesting Fact: Roses have been cultivated for thousands of years and symbolize love and beauty in many cultures.
